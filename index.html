We Provide Better

Manicure and Padicure

Hands or feet are first soaked in an aromatherapy bath, then nails are clipped and filed. Cuticles are trimmed and followed up with a relaxing massage with essential oils. Polish is applied and finished with a long-lasting topcoat.

Facial Treatment

Deep cleansing, exfoliation with steam treatment, followed by extractions, then eyebrow shaping; a de-stressing massage for the face, neck & shoulders.

Hair styling

Hair is shaped and texturized to deliver the desired look and enhance face shape and highlight features. Includes shampoo, scalp massage, cut and blowdry style. The average price for a womens hair cut is $43. The average price for a mens hair cut is $28

Makeup
